About

The person
behind the work.

I'm a senior marketing leader who's spent a decade turning partnerships into commercial engines — deals that move product, build brand, and show up in culture in ways that actually matter.

In Canada, I've been in the room for some of the country's biggest brand moments: the PS5 launch, Toronto International Film Festival, and full seasons alongside MLSE and the Blue Jays.

Globally, I've led partner strategy for McDonald's and KFC across Just Eat Takeaway's network, built media partnerships spanning 30+ markets at Kraft Heinz, and co-launched a gaming ghost kitchen with FaZe Clan across the US.

I lead with curiosity, build with intention, and measure everything that matters.

Lead With Innovation
The best marketing leaders don't wait for permission to try something new. I've built campaigns on platforms before they peaked, championed AI tools before they were mainstream, and pushed for approaches that made rooms uncomfortable — then delivered results that made them believers.
02 —
Insights First. Always.
We have more data at our fingertips than at any point in history. I've never been interested in gut-feel marketing. Every partnership I've built, every campaign I've launched, starts with a question: what does the consumer actually want? The insights lead. Everything else follows.
03 —
Make Room at the Table
The proudest moment of my career wasn't a campaign launch. It was the first time I promoted someone. I inherited them as a flight risk, coached them for 12 months, and watched them earn the promotion they'd been working toward. I measure my leadership as much by the people I've developed as the deals I've closed.
